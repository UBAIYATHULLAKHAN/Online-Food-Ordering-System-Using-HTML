The Cuisine
The main dish at the Risotteria Melotti is "RISOTTO".

Risotto
What is it?

It is traditionally made in northern Italy, prepared by cooking the rice together with other ingredients in order to transform it into a unique dish. "Risotto" is a dish that tells a story of its territory, its history and its tradition. The Rice is a product which can be mixed with infinite ingredients, such as fish, meat, vegetables, cheese and much more. Every Risotto should be made with seasonal ingredients and accompanied by a good wine of your choice.
